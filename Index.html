About Our School

Glorious Public School, Maheshra was established in 1999 and is managed by the Vidhan Welfare Society. Located in the serene rural area of Daru block, Hazaribagh, we provide a perfect environment for learning.

We offer classes from Nursery to Std. 10. As a co-educational residential school, we focus on all-round development with English as our primary medium of instruction.
